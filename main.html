DSA vs. Competitive Programming

Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) and Competitive Programming are two fundamental concepts in computer science that play significant roles in a programmer's skill set. DSA focuses on the study of various data structures (like arrays, linked lists, trees, graphs) and algorithms (such as sorting, searching, dynamic programming) that are essential for organizing and manipulating data effectively.
